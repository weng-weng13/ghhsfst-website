Great Houston High School Figure Skating Team

About Us

The Great Houston High School Figure Skating Team is a student-run, coach-supported program dedicated to athletic excellence, artistry, and teamwork. As a registered 501(c)(3) nonprofit organization, our mission is to support young athletes and expand access to figure skating opportunities across the Houston area.
